Climate Quest

Climate Quest is inspired by real challenges facing communities worldwide. Rising temperatures, extreme weather, and resource shortages affect millions every year. This game highlights the importance of sustainability, resource management, and collective action.

Did you know? Cities consume 78% of the world's energy and produce over 60% of greenhouse gases. Your actions, big or small, can make a difference.
